# Vorila

Prepare your mind before performance.

Vorila is a minimal performance preparation app designed to help you enter the right mental state before important moments.

---

## About

Vorila guides you through a simple ritual of:

- Breathing  
- Activation  
- Focus  

It is designed for athletes, students and anyone facing high-pressure situations such as competitions, exams or presentations.

The app follows principles from performance psychology, helping you reach the optimal balance between calmness and activation.

---

## Origin

Vorila was originally developed as **PoisePrep** for the Apple Swift Student Challenge 2026.

---

## Features

- Guided breathing  
- Focus techniques  
- Activation routines  
- Minimal design  
- Offline-first  
- No tracking or data collection  

---

## Philosophy

Vorila is not a meditation app.

It is a **pre-performance ritual**.

---

## Privacy

Vorila does not collect personal data.

All functionality works entirely offline and no tracking technologies are used.
